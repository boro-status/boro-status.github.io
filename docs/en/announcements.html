Subject: PayPal Maintenance

You can set up recurring payments now or when your balance is low. You’ll receive a notification from Boro system 7, 3, and 1 day before your funds run out. Please note that when you subscribe, payment will be charged upfront for one month.

Subject: Service Disruption
Date: October 19, 2024

We would like to inform you about a service disruption that occurred recently. As a result of the incident that took place on October 19th from 00:40 UTC to 06:12 UTC, there were some issues with accessing our service during this time.

We want to assure you that all data received prior to the incident has been saved in its entirety. However, data generated during the incident unfortunately was not preserved.

We have taken all necessary measures, and at this moment, the service is fully restored. Our team is committed to maintaining the stability and reliability of our service.

As a gesture of apology for the inconvenience caused, we would like to add 3 days of free access to our service to your account.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused. Your satisfaction is our top priority. We value our customers and strive to ensure the utmost convenience and reliability of our service.

Best regards, Elecard team
